About Chetan Hrebals

We are India’s Most Popular Diabetes Clinic. It’s a caring endeavor founded by Mr. Chetan Gupta, with 3 Clinics in Delhi, we have already reached out to help 31,000 diabetics.

Diabetes is a hassle-some disease. So we don’t want the added hassle of waiting for a doctor, going to a diagnostic centre, finding products in another place etc.
At Chetan Herbals, we take the stress out of diabetes. We provide everything a diabetic might need, under one roof. Right from diagnosis to speaking to a dietician, educator, diabetologist. As a patient, you don’t have to keep going from pillar to post. So life is easy and convenient for the patient.
